Thank you for reviewing DeenDawn.

WHAT IT IS: a privacy-first Islamic worship companion: prayer times, adhan notifications, Quran reader with streamed recitation, qibla compass, tasbih counter, hijri calendar, suhoor/iftar times, and a zakat calculator.

NO ACCOUNT NEEDED: there is no login, no registration, and no server-side user data. To test: open the app, choose any city manually on the first screen (location permission is optional), and every feature is immediately available.

DIFFERENTIATION (guideline 4.3(b)): DeenDawn is not a re-skin of an existing prayer app. It offers a meaningfully different, privacy-first experience that the common apps in this category do not:
- App Privacy label is "Data Not Collected": no accounts, no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no third-party trackers of any kind.
- Fully functional offline: prayer times, the complete Quran text, translation, search, qibla, tasbih, hijri calendar, and the zakat calculator all work in airplane mode (bundled on-device databases, no server dependency).
- No advertising anywhere, and no paywall on any worship feature.
- The "Ask" feature answers by deterministic full-text search over the bundled Quran and classical-text databases on-device — exact counts and verse references, phrased as verifiable facts, with no cloud AI and no generated theological content. Ruling-seeking questions are redirected to qualified scholars, never answered by the app.
- Religious text is verified byte-for-byte against pinned public sources with SHA-256 checksums; the app never generates or alters scripture.
Happy to clarify any of the above in the Resolution Center.

IN-APP PURCHASES (guideline 3.2.1): the only purchase surface is an optional developer tip ("Support DeenDawn"). It is framed strictly as supporting app development — never as charity, zakat, or sadaqah collection — and no functionality is gated behind it.

AUDIO: Quran recitation streams from our own storage; notification sounds are bundled and under 30 seconds.

LOCATION: when-in-use only, processed on-device for prayer times and qibla bearing; never transmitted. Manual city entry is a full alternative.
