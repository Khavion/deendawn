Deen Dawn is a privacy-first companion for daily worship. Free forever, with no ads, no accounts, and no tracking of any kind.

PRAYER TIMES
Accurate times for your city with all major calculation methods (ISNA, MWL, Umm al-Qura, and more), Asr madhab choice, and sensible handling of high-latitude locations. Pick your city manually or use on-device location — your position never leaves your phone.

ADHAN REMINDERS
Per-prayer notifications with your choice of sound. A rolling schedule keeps the days ahead covered, including an optional pre-Fajr suhoor reminder during Ramadan.

QURAN
The complete Quran in the Uthmani script with a readable English translation, verse-by-verse. Bookmarks, last-read position, full-text search, streaming recitation audio, and a warm night-reading mode.

ASK
Ask a question in plain words — "How many verses mention patience?" — and get exact, verifiable answers computed from the Quran text on your device, with tappable references. Questions about religious rulings are respectfully redirected to qualified scholars; Deen Dawn never issues rulings.

QIBLA
A compass that points to the Kaaba, with true-north correction and honest calibration guidance.

AND MORE
Tasbih counter with haptics and daily history. Hijri calendar with key Islamic dates (clearly labeled as calculated — moonsighting may differ). Suhoor and iftar times in Ramadan. A zakat calculator that works entirely from values you enter. A small library of public-domain works from classical Muslim thinkers.

PRIVACY, PLAINLY
No accounts. No analytics. No ad networks. Location is used on your device only. Everything essential works in airplane mode. The only network use is streaming recitation audio you request.

Deen Dawn is free and complete — an optional tip in Settings supports development, and nothing is ever locked behind it.
