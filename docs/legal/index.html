Deen Dawn

Prayer times, adhan reminders, the Quran, qibla and more — free forever, with no ads, no accounts, and no tracking of any kind.

Your worship stays on your phone.

Deen Dawn collects no data at all. No sign-in, no analytics, no ad networks. Everything essential works in airplane mode.

What's inside

  • Prayer times — all major calculation methods, Asr madhab choice, high-latitude handling.
  • Adhan reminders — per-prayer notifications, plus a pre-Fajr suhoor reminder in Ramadan.
  • Quran — Uthmani script with translation, search, bookmarks, adjustable text size, night reading.
  • Qibla — a compass to the Kaaba with true-north correction.
  • And more — tasbih counter, hijri calendar, suhoor/iftar times, zakat calculator.

Privacy, plainly

Location, if you allow it, is used on your device only and never transmitted — and you can skip it entirely by choosing your city by hand. Your settings, bookmarks and history live on your phone. Deen Dawn is free and complete; an optional tip supports development, and nothing is ever locked behind it.
